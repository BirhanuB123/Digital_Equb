How Digital Ekub Works

1. Join as Member

Register and become a member of our digital ekub community. Each member contributes 5,000 birr monthly.

2. Bi-weekly Selection

Every 2 weeks, we randomly select one lucky member to receive half of the collected amount.

3. Fair Distribution

Our system ensures all members get a chance before anyone is selected again, maintaining fairness.

Example Scenario

Monthly Collection

  • 12 members × 5,000 birr = 60,000 birr
  • Collection period: Monthly
  • Total pool: 60,000 birr

Bi-weekly Distribution

  • Round 1: 30,000 birr to lucky member
  • Round 2: 30,000 birr to lucky member
  • Fair rotation system

About Digital Ekub

Digital Ekub is a modern digital platform that brings the traditional Ethiopian Ekub system into the 21st century. We combine the community spirit of traditional ekub with cutting-edge technology to create a secure, transparent, and fair money collection and distribution system.

Our platform ensures that every member has an equal opportunity to benefit from the collective savings, while maintaining the social bonds that make ekub such a valuable tradition.
